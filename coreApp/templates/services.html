Our Services

Hydrological Surveys

Whether at State or National Level, we have the capacity to carry out a Hydrological survey(s) to exacting standards. Clients include: Private, Federal and State Governments.

Borehole Drilling

We have the capacity to drill deep boreholes from 100ft to 1500ft. we have the equipment as well as the manpower to do, perform to exacting standards. Clients include: Federal, local governments, private as well as large Institutional clients.

Rehabilitation / Maintenance

Our capability extends to major overhauling of large water treatment plants, borehole rehabilitation as well as maintaining them on a day to day level.

Pipeworks & Reticulations

Construction and Laying of pipes is one major activities and we have successfully executed major pipes laying works on behalf of Federal/Local Governments and private individuals as well as large property developers.

Fabrication & Construction of Steel Towers & Tanks

One of our strenghts is the fabrication of steel tanks and towers, from normal fabrication to assembly of braithwaite tanks.

Consultancy

Over the years we have gained so much experience in the field of water engineering and general contracting that we offer consultancy services to out clients as part of our services.
