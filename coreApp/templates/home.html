WELCOME TO STRATA & STRUCTURES LTD

The Water Solution Experts

Strata & Structures Limited is one of the leading firms in Nigeria when it comes to drilling, installation and maintenance of boreholes, water pumps, water analysis and treatment, erection of steel tank stands and tanks.

A preliminary assessment, local knowledge and/ or experience based on working close to the project site is used to increase the chance of finding water suitable for client requirements before a borehole can be drilled. Drilled wells are typically created using either top-head rotary style, table rotary, or cable tool drilling machines, all of which use drilling stems that are turned to create a cutting action in the formation, hence the term 'drilling'.

Boreholes are an effective way of tapping into the water bearing aquifer below the ground and pumping the water to the surface. Strata & Structures Limited install, service and maintain boreholes and pumping equipment for domestic use, estates, construction projects, communities, NGOS, government institutions, Hospitals, Golf clubs, farmers, and companies.
